## Logo Design

### **Logo Components**

#### 1. **Ashoka Chakra-Inspired Emblem**
The logo features a modern interpretation of the Ashoka Chakra with 24 spokes, representing:
- **24-hour service availability** (24/7 citizen support)
- **Continuity and progress** (the wheel of time and development)
- **Unity in diversity** (connecting all government services)

#### 2. **Color Coding of Spokes**
The 24 spokes feature three alternating colors:
- **Saffron (#FF9933)** - Every 3rd spoke (represents courage and sacrifice)
- **Green (#138808)** - Every 3rd spoke (represents growth and prosperity)
- **Navy (#000080)** - Every 3rd spoke (represents truth and duty)

This creates a harmonious blend of the Indian tricolor within the circular chakra.

#### 3. **Concentric Circles**
- **Outer Circle (Saffron, 10% opacity)**: Represents the broad reach of services
- **Middle Circle (Navy, stroke)**: Represents the government framework
- **Inner Circle (Navy, solid)**: Represents the core mission
- **Decorative Outer Ring (Navy, dashed)**: Represents continuous improvement

#### 4. **Typography**
Dual-script branding:
- **Primary**: ‡§∏‡•á‡§µ‡§æ ‡§∏‡§ø‡§Ç‡§ß‡•Å (Devanagari script) - connects with Hindi-speaking citizens
- **Secondary**: SEVA SINDHU (Latin script) - for pan-India and international recognition

## Accessibility Considerations

### **Color Contrast**
All logo variants meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards:
- White on Navy: 14.51:1 (AAA)
- Navy on White: 14.51:1 (AAA)
- Saffron on Navy: 4.52:1 (AA)
- Green on White: 4.54:1 (AA)

### **Screen Readers**
Logo has proper ARIA labels:
```html
<Logo aria-label="Seva Sindhu - Government of India Citizen Services Portal" />
```

### **Reduced Motion**
Logo animations can be disabled for users with vestibular disorders:
```css
@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) {
  /* Disable rotation and scale animations */
}
```

## Co-Branding Guidelines

### **With Government Emblems**
When used with:
- National Emblem (Ashoka Lion Capital)
- Digital India logo
- Make in India logo

**Placement**: Seva Sindhu logo on the right, government emblem on the left, equal height, minimum 1x logo width spacing

### **With Ministry/Department Logos**
- Seva Sindhu as primary
- Ministry logo as secondary (80% size)
- Horizontal alignment preferred

### **Prohibited Co-Branding**
